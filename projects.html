Stroop Test in Ruby

Recently at Ada Developers Academy, our cohort learned about Ruby Gems like Colorize. I decided to make a Stroop test that would be playable in Ruby!

In a Stroop test like the one I made, you have to say the name of the color that you see, rather than the word. So in the first line below, you would say: "pink green pink yellow green pink"! This is really hard on your brain, especially when the color of the word conflicts with the word itself. Try to go as fast as you can!
